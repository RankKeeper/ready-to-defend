Built on 26 Review Modules
Every report applies Jennifer Touati's proprietary dissertation methodology — the same framework used in 1-on-1 coaching.
🛡️
Research Alignment
Your problem, purpose, methodology, and research questions must form a diamond. One broken link and your committee stops the defense. We find it first.
📖
Literature Review
Gold Standard
Your literature review is the foundation of your scholarly argument. We check structure, currency, balance, and whether your gap is clearly identified and defensible.
🎯
Voice & Ownership
AI writing patterns, anthropomorphic statements, first person usage, and verbatim consistency — every flag is something your committee will catch.
⚖️
APA 7 & Citation Authority
In Chapters 1–3, you are not the expert yet. In Chapters 4–5, you are. Citation authority shifts by chapter — and committees know it. We check both directions.
🔬
Chapter 5 Integrity
Key components must be present: fulfillment of purpose, implications for practice and research, reconnecting with and demonstrating you understand the results of your study.
✍️
AI Detection
Formulaic transitions, excessive hedging, repetitive parallel structure — we flag AI writing patterns. Your committee will notice. We help you address it before they do.

Founder, Ready To Defend  |  Education Under Construction LLC
My pain became my passion. After my own EdD journey in Educational Leadership and Curriculum, I founded Education Under Construction in 2018 to coach doctoral candidates through the dissertation process. When AI entered the picture in 2023, I paused — and listened. What I heard from students was clear: they didn't need more help writing. They needed help understanding and defending what they'd written.

Ready To Defend was built for that student. The one who has a draft but doesn't know if they can defend every word. The one who used AI and panicked. The one who needs a committee-ready dissertation — not just a finished one.
